### ‚ö†Ô∏è RECOMMENDATION: Disable Private WiFi Address

**Why disable it on YOUR network:**
1. **Device tracking** - Can't track device history consistently
2. **DHCP exhaustion** - Each MAC gets new IP, wastes DHCP pool
3. **Firewall rules** - Can't create device-specific rules
4. **Parental controls** - Can't enforce time limits
5. **Network troubleshooting** - Harder to identify devices

**How to disable on iOS:**
1. Settings ‚Üí Wi-Fi
2. Tap (i) next to "darkstar" network
3. Toggle OFF "Private Wi-Fi Address"
4. Reconnect to network

**Trade-off:**
- ‚úÖ Better network management
- ‚ùå Less privacy when joining PUBLIC WiFi networks

**Recommendation:** 
- ‚úÖ Disable for YOUR home network
- ‚úÖ Keep enabled for public/untrusted networks
